Green Curry

Image:

Reference: Bucknall Megan. "Green Curry", Published 14, 2020, Unsplash.com, https://unsplash.com/photos/green-soup-on-white-ceramic-bowl-qav5LFLbSUk

[A] ingredients:

  1. "Thai basil
  2. Bamboo shoots, canned
  3. Red bell pepper
  4. Green curry paste
  5. Makrut lime leaves
  6. Full fat coconut milk
  7. Fish sauce
  8. Palm sugar
  9. Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  10. Chicken stock, unsalted"

    11. (Chongchitnant)

[B] How to:

  • "Pound green curry paste and Thai basil leaves for an extra vibrant colour.
  • Get the paste as fine as possible.
  • Reduce the coconut milk until very thick
  • Add the curry paste and saute for a few minutes.
  • Add fish sauce and palm sugar.
  • Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is fork tender.
  • Add bamboo shoots and cook for a minute until the curry comes back to a boil.
  • Stir in red bell pepper and turn off the heat.
  • Add Thai basil and stir just until wilted.
  • The green curry is now ready to serve with jasmine rice!"

    • (Chongchitnant)

    Reference [A],[B]: Chongchitnant, Pailin. “Green Thai Curry Recipe.” Hot Thai Kitchen, 23 April 2022, https://hot-thai-kitchen.com/green-curry-new-2/. Accessed 23 October 2023.

    Image:

    Reference: Ng, Vicky (@vickyng). "Bim Bim Bab", Published May 24, 2020, Unsplash.com, https://unsplash.com/photos/cooked-food-on-white-ceramic-bowls-diDja86YL_M

    [C] ingredients:

    1. "...Beef mince,
    2. ...Soy sauce,
    3. ...Sesame oil,
    4. ...Brown sugar,
    5. ...Minced garlic,
    6. ...Seasoned spinach,
    7. ...Seasoned bean sprouts,
    8. ...Shiitake mushroom,
    9. ...Carrots,
    10. ...Sea salt
    11. ...Steamed rice
    12. ...Eggs"
    13. etc.

      14. (Sue from mykoreankitchen.com)

    [D] How to:

  • "Prepare and cook ingredients as below...
  • Mix the bibimbap sauce ingredients in a bowl.
  • Cook spinach and bean sprouts per linked recipe.
  • Rinse, peel and julienne the carrots. Add some cooking oil and 1/4 tsp of fine sea salt in a wok and cook the carrots on medium high to high heat for 2 to 3 mins.
  • Clean/rinse the shiitake mushrooms and thinly slice them. Add some cooking oil and 1/4 tsp of fine sea salt in a wok and cook the mushrooms on medium high to high heat until they are all cooked. (It takes 2 to 3 mins.)
  • Make fried eggs. (While sunny side up is common, you can make them per your preference.)
  • Put the rice into a bowl and add the meat, assorted vegetables, seasoned seaweed, bibimbap sauce, and the egg on top of the rice. Serve"

    • (Sue from mykoreankitchen.com)

    Reference [C],[D]: -, Sue. “Bibimbap (Korean Mixed Rice with Meat and Assorted Vegetables).” My Korean Kitchen, 3 January 2022, https://mykoreankitchen.com/bibimbap-korean-mixed-rice-with-meat-and-assorted-vegetables/. Accessed 23 October 2023.
